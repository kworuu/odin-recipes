Bicol Express

About The Recipe

Bicol Express is one of the spiciest dishes of the Philippines. Tender pork is stewed in a rich,
savory coconut sauce, making it perfect alongside a bowl of rice and fresh vegetables. This is the classic comfort food amongst Filipinos!


    Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb pork belly
  • 2 cups thin coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoon fresh shrim paste (alamang)
  • 10 pieces Thai chili pepper
  • 6 pieces siling haba (Filipino chili)
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cup thick coconut cream
  • 3 tablespoon minced ginger
  • Salt and pepper

    Instructions

  1. Heat a pan and pour in the cooking oil.
  2. Saute the onion, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Add the pork and cook until the coor is light brown.
  4. Mix the coconut milk and alamang
  5. Bring to a boil, lower the heat, and then let it simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Add chili peppers and cook until half the liquid has evaporated
  7. Add the siling haba.
  8. Pour in the coconut cream, and continue to cook until oil comes out from the cream.
  9. aAdd salt and pepper for taste.
  10. Serve hot.
